Delicious chocolate Cake

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 157 calories; protein 2.3g; carbohydrates 25.7g; fat 5.7g; cholesterol 16.3mg; sodium 217.1mg

Ingredients.

  • 2 cups white sugar.
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour.
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder.
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 1 cup milk.
  • ½ cup vegetable oil.
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.
  • 1 cup boiling water.

Steps.

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour two nine inch round pans
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla
  5. Mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer.
  6. Stir in the boiling water ( Batter will be thin).
  7. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  8. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven.
  9. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes
  10. Move to a wire rack to cool completely.
